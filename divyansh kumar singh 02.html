MY Gallery and Rules

Materials Needed for Your Own Art
  • Paper and canvas
  • Paints (Acrylic or Watercolor)
  • Brushes
  • Water

Steps to Create Your Own Drawing
  1. Prepare your workspace.
  2. Sketch your idea on the canvas.
  3. Start painting with the background colors as per your choice.
  4. Add the details.
  5. Let it dry and sign your work!
